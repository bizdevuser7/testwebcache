# Radio and escalation

![World Cup 2026 Vancouver host city brand](vancouver-host-city-brand.jpg)

Radios are shared operational space. Speak only when you have a specific request, location, or status update.

## Standard call format

1. Say who you are calling, then identify yourself: `North Lead, this is Gate 1 queue support`.
2. Give the location first: `Gate A3, outside security screening`.
3. State the action needed: `Need one accessibility escort for a guest using a mobility device`.

## Escalate immediately

- Medical distress, heat illness, slips, falls, or suspected concussion.
- Security threats, prohibited items, crowd pressure, or blocked emergency access.
- Missing child, separated support person, or vulnerable guest without a companion.
