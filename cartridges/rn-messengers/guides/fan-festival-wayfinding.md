# Fan Festival wayfinding

![World Cup 2026 Vancouver host city brand](vancouver-host-city-brand.jpg)

The Fan Festival Vancouver runs at the PNE Amphitheatre. Crew members should help guests move from transit, bike parking, rideshare, and pedestrian routes without creating new pinch points.

## First answer

- Point with an open hand and name the next landmark, not the whole route.
- Send guests to official entrances only, even if a shortcut looks open.
- Remind guests that bag checks and screening happen before the festival area.

## Common questions

- Transit: direct guests to the signed shuttle and bus stops on Hastings Street.
- Rideshare: use the marked pickup and drop-off area; do not stop vehicles in the pedestrian approach.
- Accessibility: call the accessibility lead if a guest needs step-free routing, seating support, or a quiet handoff.
- Lost items: send valuables and found bags to the information tent, then log the location and time.

## End of shift

1. Return handheld signs and radios to the crew desk.
2. Report damaged signs, blocked routes, or repeated guest confusion.
3. Share one improvement for the next shift briefing.
