The Testing Process

What is a Cellular Nutrition Assay (CNA)? +
A Cellular Nutrition Assay measures nutrients inside your cells — not just what's circulating in your blood. Standard blood tests measure serum levels (the fluid around your cells). A CNA goes deeper, measuring what's actually fueling your cells where metabolism happens. You can have normal serum levels and still have your cellular performance limited. The CNA reveals the complete picture — 30+ vitamins, minerals, amino acids, antioxidants, and metabolites at the intracellular level.
How does the blood draw work? +
A licensed phlebotomist collects your blood sample — included in your $499 test kit price. AW2 coordinates the phlebotomy service for you. It's a simple blood draw, quick and straightforward. You don't need to go to a lab or arrange anything yourself. Everything is handled through AW2.
Who runs the cellular testing behind AW2? +
AW2 partners with a CLIA-certified, FDA-inspected, and internationally accredited specialty clinical laboratory — one of the most credentialed cellular testing facilities in the world. AW2 is everything else.
